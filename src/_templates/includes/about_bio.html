I'm Tifa Jade, but you can call me TJ.

I’m a Creative Director and Producer with a deep love for executing ambitious ideas, directing animation that impresses, and telling stories that move people.

My creative journey began when I was very young, making stop-motion films with toys. Later, as a student filmmaker I would first discover my leadership abilities — inspiring teams to work together to create stories that hit on a human level and won awards at film festivals.

That same spirit led me to my first leadership role as Creative Services Director at a small NBC affiliate in Eastern Washington. There, I worked directly with local businesses to develop campaigns, direct commercials, design graphics, and shape brand identities from scratch.

But I wanted to tell deeper stories. I pitched and solo-produced a documentary series about a high school football team, which aired on regional television and landed me a role at UFC, the biggest combat sports promotion in the world. There, I spent four years telling stories at the highest level of sports — directing features, shaping campaigns, and working with global partners to bring heart to one of the most intense sports in the world.

Eventually, I combined my creative background with my love of gaming, stepping into a Creative Producer role at BoomTV, an esports-focused, experiential marketing agency serving gaming and tech clients. There, I shaped creative identities for 400+ branded events and campaigns, directing trailers, designing graphics, and shaping content strategy — with over 100 million viewers seeing my work through high-profile clients like Xbox, Fortnite, and Mountain Dew.

Today, I lead creative projects through my agency, Tiny Pond, and continue to design and produce esports events at In Queue, an independent gaming events brand, as well as taking on freelance work for creators and musicians. While I’ve worked with some of the biggest names in sports and entertainment, at the end of the day, it’s all about the craft. I love bringing ambitious ideas to life, developing workflows that solve problems, and pushing the limits of what's possible.

If you're building something bold and need a creative partner who thrives on pressure and lives for the details — let's talk; I'd love to hear from you.
